Performs the primality test based on Lucas sequence.

See LucasCheck for possible checks, and the implementors of LucasBase for the corresponding bases.

When used with SelfridgeBase and LucasCheck::Regular, implements the algorithm prescribed by the FIPS.186-5 standard1.
